About the Company

Orientertainment consists of a humble team of developers creating games for various audiences. So far, we have made 3 games since this company has started out, and it is with great delight to say that many more are on the way! Besides that, we also offer subscription services for interested gamers who want to gain access to multiple perks and benefits that we currently have on offer.

Why create this company?

Orientertainment started with a dream, a dream to bring entertainment to everyone interested in games as much as we do. It is a dream to develop great games proudly made in the Philippines. A couple years in, we the team behind Orientertainment have made quite a ways to getting closer to that dream, and we will not stop until we achieve it!

Freqeuntly Asked Questions

Where can I buy games?

We sell games both physically and digitally. You can go to your nearest entertainment retail store and get our games there! Or, you could go to your preferred gaming device's online store to search for our products!

Are the games on console or PC?

Both! We makes games for everyone! Even on mobile devices you could find our games there!

Any plans on branching out to other products/services?

Yes, we have plans! Though it may take some time... we plan to broaden our horizons when we finally have the resources to do so!
